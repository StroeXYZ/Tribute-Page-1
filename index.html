Biography

Stephen Hawking was a theoretical physicist
and cosmologist,widely considered to be one
of the greatest scientists of his time.
He was the first scientist to devise a
cosmology that married the general theory of
relativity and quantum mechanics, and he
made huge contributions to our understanding
of black holes. Hawking wrote a number of
popular science books including the
bestseller A Brief History of Time.
